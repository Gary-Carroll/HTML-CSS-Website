www.burnsidehyd.ie/

Burnside Hydracyl is a factory located in Carlow that designs and manufactures hydraulic cylinders. I worked there for 15 months, between graduating from my undergraduate course and starting the masters I'm currently doing. I programmed and operated two computer numerical control (CNC) milling machines, a Daewoo Ace V430 and a Daewoo Ace H400P. I also trained others in the programming and use of these two machines. I made many different parts used in the assembly of hydraulic cylinders, such as cylinder ends, cylinder bottoms, valve blocks and port blocks. This was done using a huge variety of tools, such as drills, slot drills, chamfer tools, milling cutters, U-drills, form tools, reamers, boring bars, taps, spotface tools and end mills.
